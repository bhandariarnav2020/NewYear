Happy New Year To All

New Year is celebrated all over the world with great enthusiasm and fun. Earlier, it was a Roman calendar which was having only ten months and designated 1st march as the new year. In the Gregorian calendar, there are 12 months in every year and the new year falls on January 1st which is widely accepted and celebrated the festival.

Celebrations

It is a special day for the people as this day is the beginning of the new year and they welcome the upcoming year in their way. People start the preparations for the new year celebration many days earlier. People buy new clothes, gifts and many new things from the market. Shops are full of the crowd these days. On the midnight of 31st December and on 1st January there is a celebration of New Year in India and all over the world.

Communities

Though different communities celebrate their new year on different days according to their calendar, then everyone celebrates the new year on 1st January and this is a festival for all the community people. And people spread joy and happiness in nature.
